About

I am Dr. Phan Huynh Anh, Director of Smentor Institute and lecturer at the Faculty of International Business, University of Economics Ho Chi Minh City (UEH). I specialize in soft skills, entrepreneurship, marketing, and mentoring, with a mission to bridge education and business practice. I am the creator of the “Enterprise Semester” program – a practical training model for students, connecting them directly with businesses.

“Practical education is the key for students to integrate and succeed.”
